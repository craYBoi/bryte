Brown CareerLAB

The Story

In May 2016, CareerLAB, the career services center at Brown University, did a pilot shoot with Bryte to take headshots for Brown University students. To advertise the shoot, CareerLAB posted once on their Facebook Page and sent out a single email to Brown Undergraduates. The response was unbelievable: in 94 minutes all 40 headshots on the Bryte website were booked. The next 24 hours saw 208 email signups from students requesting waitlist spots for the shoot. Between the exciting response from students, and an excellent solution provided by Bryte, CareerLAB proceeded to purchase another 60 headshots in May 2016, and also hired Bryte for 5 more shoots during the Fall 2016 Semester.

The Process

Each student had 3 minutes to take a headshot, the standard time for Bryte headshots at career service centers. The headshots were taken by Bryte photographer, Sarah Morse. Sarah is a RISD student with freelance photography experience. She helped the clients choose poses on photo day and also touched up the headshots after the shoot.
