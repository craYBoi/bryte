Taking photos with Bryte

Bryte Photo was started by two Brown students who weren’t happy with the LinkedIn photo options available to them. They set out to create a better LinkedIn experience for students and designed a “pose-flow” and camera equipment setup that produced amazing LinkedIn photos. After offering this experience on Brown’s Main Green, the founders received hundreds of requests around campus for their LinkedIn photos. Now, they’ve grown to other schools and teach their LinkedIn photo-taking formula to keen photographers.

Free equipment

We provide you with the lighting and backdrop equipment you’ll need to take LinkedIn photos. You’ll get a golf-bag-sized duffel bag to carry it to and from photoshoots. To become a Bryte Photographer, all you need is your own camera and tripod!
