Apply to become a Bryte Student Photographer!

Basic Qualification

  1. Expect around 30 hours of commitment per semester (24 hours on photoshoot, 4 hours in training, 2 hours in followup)
  2. Willing to commute to other schools in nearby area, gas/uber fee will be covered
  3. Will be able to commit 5 hours straight on given dates
  4. Has a relatively flexible schedule and will be available during school major career fairs
  5. Be very responsive to trainier/shoot organizer
  6. Be responsible, friendly and confident
  7. Ability to understand the gist of taking good headshot photos from training and practice
  8. Ability to learn from previous experiences

Summary of Key Responsibilities

  1. Goes through Bryte Training program (2 in person training, online quiz, post training practices, and post-shoot followup with your assigned trainer
  2. Communicates with shoot organizer to confirm on the shoot dates weeks before the actual photoshoot
  3. Shows up at Bryte photoshoot on given dates at given locations, set up the equipment and take amazing headshot photos for students
  4. Upload the headshot photos to Dropbox after the shoot
  5. Be punctual, responsible and friendly

Required Knowledge

  1. Basic Knowledge of DSLR and its basic settings (Focus, Aperture, Shutter Speed, ISO, white balance..)
