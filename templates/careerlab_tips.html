We're going to make you look really good!

What should I wear?

  • There are lots of different ways to look professional in your photos. We say: wear whatever you're most comfortable in! You'll have a surefire look in your photo when you're wearing your favorite blazer. Look at some of these great outfits we've seen at past photoshoots.

    • Before you take your photos:

  • Arrive at least 5 min before your timeslot begins so you can have time to brush up in the waiting room with the mirror and other tools we provide. Here are some things to look for when brushing yourself up:

  • ladies

    • Resting your jacket, blouse, or sweater evenly across your shoulders
    • Last minute hair care (you may bring a brush or a comb)

  • gentlemen

    • You may wear a tie, jacket, or button down shirt. If you’re wearing a tie, look to keep it centered at the top of your collar.
    • Resting your shirt or jacket smoothly along your shoulders
    • Last minute hair care (you may bring a brush or comb)

We know how to make you smile :)))

  • We'll take you through different types of poses so you find the one that's uniquely best for you. It's important to relax here and let our photographer guide you through the poses. It's going to be super comfortable.

How does the process work?

  • Head to the waiting room when you get to the photoshoot. You can brush up there like we talked about above.
  • You'll take a photo with a whiteboard to start. We call this the "mugshot". We have you take this photo because the identifying number we write on the whiteboard will be how we know to attach your photos to your email.
  • Then your photographer will guide you through all of the poses we designed to make you look amazingly professional.
  • Shortly after the photoshoot you'll be emailed a link to a provide gallery with all your photos. This is where you can download them. Once you download a photo you can use it however you want!
