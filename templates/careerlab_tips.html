Bryte Photo Headshot Tips

Before your Headshot

  • Please arrive at the shoot location 5 min. before you time slot begins
  • Sit down in the waiting area until your photographer is ready to shoot your headshot. Photos will be taken on a first come, first serve basis for each time slot.
  • We will have grey and white backdrop options available on shoot day. We think both look good, and allow you to pick your favorite on shoot day.
  • You can use the mirror in the waiting area to prepare for your headshot. Here are some ways the mirror is useful:

  • Ladies

    • Resting your jacket, blouse, or sweater evenly across your shoulders

During your Bryte Photo Headshot

1. When it’s time to take your photo, your photographer will hand you a whiteboard with your unique number written on it. We give you this number so we can identify you when we send you your touched up photo.

2. You’ll take one photo with this whiteboard at the beginning of your session. You can hand it to your photographer after you take the photo.

3. You’re photographer a couple different poses on shoot day. Throughout your session, you’ll be able to see your photos as you take them. We’ll make sure you walk out of the session with one you like.

4. After you’ve had your session, you’re all done! We’ll touch up your favorite photo within 48 hours. Then we’ll email you a link to a Dropbox folder that has you a touched up and raw version of the photo.
